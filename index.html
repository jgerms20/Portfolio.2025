About Me

My journey began in Columbia, South Carolina, where Southern storytelling shaped my creative lens. Now, as a Senior Connections Strategist at TBWA\Chiat\Day in Los Angeles, I bring a global mindset to my work, honed at agencies like Wieden + Kennedy and Goodby, Silverstein & Partners.

A University of South Carolina grad (B.A. Mass Communications, 2020), I'm driven by curiosity, community, DE&I, and cutting-edge tech like AI and Web3. My work has earned a 2024 Cannes Titanium Grand Prix, but my mission is connecting people, ideas, and culturesalways with a story worth telling.

Brand Work / Levi's REIIMAGINE

Levi's x Beyonc REIIMAGINE Campaign

Role Lead Strategic Planner
Agency TBWA\Chiat\Day
Year 2024-2025
Scroll to explore

Campaign Overview

The Levi's REIIMAGINE campaign with Beyonc is a bold celebration of the brand's legacy as a canvas for self-expression, reimagining iconic ads for a modern era. Inspired by Beyonc's track "LEVII'S JEANS" from her critically acclaimed album COWBOY CARTER, the campaign unfolds in chapters, each reinterpreting a classic Levi's advertisement. I led the strategy at TBWA\Chiat\Day, focusing on connecting with fans through a global, multi-channel approach. The campaign sparked $65M+ in earned media within 48 hours, proving Levi's remains the definitive denim lifestyle brand.

Impressions

Chapter 1: Launderette

A modern take on Levi's iconic 1985 ad, featuring Beyonc in a launderette setting that celebrates timeless style and confidence.

Release Date September 2024
Director Melina Matsoukas

Chapter 2: Pool Hall

Reimagining another classic Levi's moment with Beyonc's signature style and confidence in a pool hall setting.

Release Date February 2025
Director Melina Matsoukas

Chapter 3: Refrigerator

The latest chapter takes the campaign to a sun-drenched roadside diner, reimagining the original 1988 Refrigerator ad with Beyonc moving with confidence and ready to take on her next challenge.

Release Date April 2025
Director
Special Guest Willie Jones

Chapter 4: The Denim Cowboy

Final installment of the yearlong Levis REIIMAGINE campaign weaving together prior chapters and introducing the BEYONC x LEVIS denim collection.

Published August 2025

Through "REIIMAGINE" and our partnership with Beyonc, one of the most influential artists of our time, Levi's is building on decades of classic iconography and celebrating what it means to lead with intent and confidence. The campaign, which reimagines iconic Levi's ads, has generated over $65 million in estimated earned media value and more than 4.3 billion impressions. This powerful collaboration has significantly impacted the brand's performance, contributing to an 8% increase in Levi's global organic sales and a notable 19.87% increase in foot traffic to Levi's stores across the United States. The campaign demonstrates that Levi's isn't just a legacy brandit's a brand with an incredible heritage that consistently remains relevant and influential in global culture.
